Our Story

An Australian family business with a national mission

"To provide and improve access to dental services for all Australians, nationwide."
— MDCA Mission Statement

Mobile Dental Clinics Australia (MDCA) is a wholly Australian owned family business providing mobile emergency, general and dental laboratory services throughout Australia.

MDCA was founded in 2015 by Caitlin Amy Wainrib (1989–2020), an Oral Health Therapist and Hygienist with extended scope qualifications. Her career spanned over 10 years in private practices across Australia and the United Kingdom, as well as with Monash Health.

During her time with the Victorian Domiciliary Dental Service, Caitlin developed a passion for meeting the needs of those less able to access traditional dental clinics. Starting with a single mobile unit and van — while continuing to lecture at Melbourne and LaTrobe Universities — she built MDCA into the nationwide fleet it is today.
